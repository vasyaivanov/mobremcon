What is it?

SLITE is a web portal for your presentations: remote presentations to someone in another city and regular conference room presentations right from the web.

How does it work?

1.      Go to www.slite.us and upload your presentation (PowerPoint file (.pptx or .ppt))

2.      Slite with generate a unique 4 digit code for your presentation (note: it is case sensitive)

a.      You will get a unique web page (url) with that hash, after upload finishes.

3.      Share this url with anyone in the world.

4.      Click on menu ☰ in the upper right hand corner and chose what you want to do.

5.      When you will Present everyone in the world looking at this web page (url) will see same thing live: presentation going to next slide, laser pointer, chat, video and so on

Present

1.      Upload your presentation and get a unique web page address (url) as described in How does it work?

2.      Click menu ☰ and chose Become Presenter

a.      This will open another window which is your Remote Control

3.      See Remote Control section below for explanation how it works.

Remote Control

Remote Control allows you to control the presentation for anyone in the world looking at the web page with your presentation live.

There are 3 ways to get remote control:

1.      From your presentation web page -> click menu ☰ ->  click Become Presenter

2.      Go to www.slite.us/remote and enter 4 digit code of your presentation (you can see 4 digit code in the address bar of your web page)

a.      Note: you can do it from your mobile device (phone) and youll get a cool remote control on your mobile simply from entering this url in the browser on your phone.

3.      Get free SliteRemoteControl iPhone application from Apple App Store.

a.      For now it works only on iPhone, but Android and Windows Phone versions are planned soon.

What can I do with Remote Control?

1.      Next or Previous will move slides of your presentation for everyone! who is looking at it on the web.

2.      Pointer is a laser pointer. Click in Pointer button to turn it on or off. You click and move your mouse (or your finger on your phone) and everyone will see the dot highlighting a spot on the slide.

3.      Draw you can draw on any slide and everyone will see it. To turn it on simply click on Draw button.

4.      Time time how long your presentation is going. To start simply click on Start button. To stop simply click on running numbers.

Chat

You can chat with anyone in the world looking at this presentation. This conversation will stay forever, even after you done with your presentation. You can continue discussion after the presentation is done and come back a month from now to see what was said.

To open a chat inside your presentation click on menu ☰ ->  click Chat

Personal Notes

Take notes about presentation right inside the presentation! No one except for you can see those notes. They are private.

To open Personal Notes inside your presentation click on menu ☰ ->  click Personal Notes

Video Chat

Video chat right inside the presentation with remote participants! Or, if you are presenter, compliment slides with live video stream of you presenting.

To open Video Chat inside your presentation click on menu ☰ ->  click Video Chat
