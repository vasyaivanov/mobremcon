About Our Product

PREZERA.COM™ - live online presentations.

PREZERA.COM™ enables you to present from inside your browser to a remote and in-house audiences. It comes with an iPhone remote control app. PREZERA.COM™ adds a social component inside your presentation allowing audience to connect with each other and presenter. Desktop sharing and video broadcasting make your presentation efficient. PREZERA.COM™ is the best and the simpliest way in the world to explain information from one person to many.

How It Works

  1. Upload your existing PowerPoint presentation to our main page OR create a new online presentation via our website in minutes!

  2. Obtain the unique 4 digit code in which you can use to enter into the browser AND share the slideshow with everyone you want to show your presentation to.

  3. Now you can share your presentation LIVE by sending a unique URL, viewable by anyone in the world - all you need is a browser!

  4. use mobile app remote control to: move to next/previous slide, have a virtual laser pointer, draw on slides from your phone, and just for fun: voice control your presentation!

  5. poll your audience live. Get feedback immediately on your topic during the presentation.

Cool things you can do during your presentation:
